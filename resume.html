  • Milk Quality Technical Support
    Conaprole, Uruguay

I worked for Conaprole (largest dairy industry in Uruguay) in Florida (Uruguay) advising dairy farmers about milk quality and mastitis control in dairy farms.

  • Research Assistant
    Universidad de la República, Uruguay
